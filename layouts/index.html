About the session

Find out what fellowship at RCH is really like

This inaugural town hall is a chance to hear directly from current and recent fellows, meet members of the consultant body, and ask the questions that don't get answered on a job ad. Whether you are weighing up a 6 or 12 month fellowship, considering a PICU or NICU rotation, or simply curious about life as a paediatric anaesthesia fellow at RCH, you are warmly invited to join us.

We will share an overview of the fellowship structure, two short personal stories from recent fellows, and then break into smaller rooms so you can ask questions in a more informal setting before regrouping for a final discussion.

Program

Running order on the night

A 60 minute session with breakout rooms, structured to leave room for your questions.

  1. 5 min

    Welcome & introductions

    Session etiquette, introduction to the panel and how the evening will run.

  2. 5 min

    Fellow story

    A short personal reflection from a recent fellow.

  3. 10 min

    Fellowship overview & FAQs

    Structure of the 6 and 12 month fellowships, PICU and NICU rotations, and the most common questions answered up front.

  4. 5 min

    Fellow story

    A second personal reflection from a current or recent fellow.

  5. 20 min

    Breakout rooms

    Smaller, informal rooms with members of the panel — your chance to ask the questions you actually want answered.

  6. 10 min

    Final discussion

    Reconvene as a whole group to share themes and address any remaining questions.

  7. 5 min

    Further opportunities & close

    Leadership, research and education opportunities beyond clinical fellowship, and how to stay in touch.
